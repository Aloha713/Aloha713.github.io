I am an M.S. student in Mechanical Engineering and a graduate researcher at Ajou University. I am interested in how soft and deformable systems move, adapt, and self-organize under nonequilibrium conditions, driven by energy input, environmental changes, and interactions with their surroundings.

Since my undergraduate years, I have built research experience through experiments, image-based analysis, and physical interpretation. I enjoy starting from subtle observations, developing the tools needed to describe them, and connecting complex behaviors back to simple mechanical reasoning.

Rather than being defined by a single material or research topic, I am interested in developing a flexible research approach that integrates experiments, quantitative analysis, and physical reasoning across different soft-matter systems.
